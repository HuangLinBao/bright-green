About Us

Bright Green Lighting offers more than 2 years experience in the Middle East.
The founders of Bright Green lighting along with the Engineering Team have a global network in the lighting field. From suppliers and manufactures to loyal recurring customers.
Passion and preparation are the key factors to the growth of Istanbul Lighting. The goals and demands in the variety of projects are not only challenging but also rewarding when achieved.
People make a successful organization. Bright Green Lighting has a team of motivated light professionals to offer a multitude of light related services to find the best result for your projects.

In client meetings the solution is presented and discussed, and a commercial proposal to supply the lights on site is provided. Bright Green Lighting also commits to assistance during the installation of the lights.
Our lighting history belongs to Many years history of lighting success story in projects all over middle east where company roots belong to the Grand Father of The founder Eng. Abdo Basha , who starts lighting business in Syria in 1972 since that start the father and grandfather lighting experience moved with the next generation over the GCC in Dubai , Abu Dhabi , Sharjah To apply the lighting history experience in UAE project Grooving along years of lighting experience all over middle east projects .

Our Main concern is our customers satisfaction which is leading to the main growth of the company from a small organization twenty years to one of the biggest Lighting projects Supplier over Middle East right now. We will be happy to hear from you for all concern or interest you may have.
